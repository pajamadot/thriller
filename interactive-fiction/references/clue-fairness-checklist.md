# Clue Fairness Checklist

Use this checklist when an interactive thriller route is supposed to feel playable and logically earned.

## Clue Contract

- Every clue referenced in node or choice metadata exists in `systems/clues.yaml`.
- Critical clues describe evidence, not conclusions.
- If an ending or reveal depends on a clue, that support is visible in the clue ledger.

## Route Fairness

- A route never lands on a reveal node while missing one of that node's required clues.
- Shared endings only require clues surfaced on every route that can reach them.
- Route-specific texture may differ, but route-specific texture must not be mistaken for mandatory evidence.

## Causal Truth

- Method, timeline, and motive each have at least one inspectable support path.
- No accusation is stronger than the evidence currently visible to the player.
- Early bad endings fail because of pressure or timing, not because the story hides required evidence off-route.

## Replay Value

- At least one route preserves suspicion through a different behavioral clue set.
- A silent or patient choice gains a later callback, not just a delayed ending.
- Red-team notes name the next likely fairness gap created by the current fix.
