# Suspicion Ecology Checklist

Use this checklist when a thriller route should keep multiple suspect theories alive without turning into noise.

## Cast Layer

- At least three major suspects are legible in `cast/characters.yaml`.
- Each major suspect has a distinct `thrillerProfile.pressureStyle`.
- Each suspect can be read in both innocent and dangerous ways.

## Node Layer

- Reachable scenes keep at least three `thriller.suspicionTargets` in circulation.
- Investigation or social beats expose `theorySeeds` instead of assuming the reader will infer every theory alone.
- A shared node does not collapse the suspect field back to a single obvious culprit unless that collapse is the point.

## Interaction Layer

- Choice sets let the player pursue different suspect theories, not just different tones.
- At least one theory branch ends in a live social reaction, not only a clue ledger update.
- At least one alternative theory remains plausible after the strongest current reveal.

## Red-Team Layer

- A hostile reading can name the obvious-suspect trap in one sentence.
- The current route has a visible defense against that trap.
- The next band identifies which suspect or theory still lacks texture.
